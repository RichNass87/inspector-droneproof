# DroneProof Pilot Safety Checklist

Run this before importing, uploading, or starting a drone mission.

- Confirm legal pilot status and operating rules.
- Check controlled airspace, TFRs, LAANC/authorization, local restrictions, and weather.
- Confirm visual line of sight, takeoff/landing zone, people, vehicles, trees, wires, chimneys, and neighboring property.
- Confirm GPS lock, compass, IMU, battery, propellers, firmware, storage, and camera settings.
- Confirm return-to-home altitude clears all roof features, trees, and wires.
- Review every waypoint on the drone-app map before upload.
- Do not fly a route generated from relative/non-GPS waypoints.
- Abort if any person, vehicle, animal, weather, or aircraft hazard enters the operation area.
