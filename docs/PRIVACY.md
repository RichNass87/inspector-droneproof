# Inspector DroneProof privacy policy

Effective: July 16, 2026

Inspector DroneProof is operated by Inspector Roofing and Restoration. The application supports roof-documentation jobs, field-photo intake, annotations, mission planning, QA notes, and report exports.

## Data the application may process

- Property and job details entered by an authorized user, including an address.
- Roof photos, photo metadata, roof-plane labels, annotations, and field notes.
- Mission-planning values such as coordinates, altitude, heading, camera pitch, and checklist status.
- A WordPress endpoint and field-access token stored on the configured Android device.
- Technical request information normally recorded by the website host, such as time, IP address, and user agent.

## Why data is used

Data is used to load and synchronize authorized roof-documentation jobs, upload photos, generate documentation, troubleshoot the service, protect endpoints, and support human review.

## Optional service providers

If enabled by the site administrator, an address may be sent to Google Maps Platform for geocoding and selected documentation text may be sent to the configured OpenAI service for QA assistance. WordPress hosting processes stored jobs and uploads. Each provider applies its own terms and privacy practices.

## Sharing and sale

Inspector Roofing does not sell DroneProof job data. Data may be disclosed to service providers that operate the configured workflow, to an authorized customer or project participant, when required by law, or to protect users and systems.

## Retention and deletion

WordPress records remain until deleted by an authorized administrator or under the applicable business retention process. Device settings remain until cleared, the application is uninstalled, or the device is reset. Requests for access, correction, or deletion may be submitted through https://inspector-roofing.com/contact/ and are handled subject to legal, security, and recordkeeping requirements.

## Security

Access tokens and API keys are not included in the public repository. No system can guarantee absolute security. Field tokens should be rotated if exposed or if a configured device is lost.

## Children

DroneProof is a contractor field-documentation tool and is not directed to children.

## Boundaries

DroneProof does not make insurance coverage, payment, engineering, legal, or flight-safety decisions. Inspector Roofing documents observable conditions and does not act as a public adjuster. Carriers decide coverage, payment, and claim outcomes.
